I am delighted to announce our paper [MatchFixAgent: Language-Agnostic Autonomous Repository-Level Code Translation Validation and Repair](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.16187) has been accepted to ICML 2026. This work is a collaboration between UIUC [Intelligent CAT Lab](https://reyhaneh.cs.illinois.edu/lab.htm) and AWS. Looking forward to presenting it in Seoul, South Korea 🇰🇷
